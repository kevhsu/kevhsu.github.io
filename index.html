Summit

Smash Summit is a very prestigious tournament series that features 16 of the best players in the world in Super Smash Bros Melee. Each tournament has a vote-in system with votes earned from purchases/donations that fund the prize pool and tournament costs.

How to Help

Swedish needs the most votes on Saturday October 20th to make it into Smash Summit 7.
The most helpful way to contribute is by registering on smash.gg, making a small purchase, and using your votes to contribute to Swedish!
If you have a twitch account, you can get an extra 15 votes by subscribing to the Summit Channel as instructed on the smash.gg link above
We also really appreciate financial support via direct donations to James.
You can also venmo @james-liu-16. We recommend a $15 donation, but any amount that you can spare would be greatly appreciated.

Swedish Delight

James "Swedish Delight" Liu is in the voting stage of Smash Summit 7. In 2015, Swedish was ranked #31 in the global rankings before attending the very first Smash Summit as a vote-in competitor. This experience was crucial to his improvement to #11 on the 2016 rankings and #16 on the 2017 rankings. His goal is to finish 2018 in the top 10, and another Summit experience would be critical to making that happen.
